    Abhishek Verma

    I’m a seasoned Java and Spring Boot Developer with over 7 years of experience crafting enterprise-level applications on the Spring stack. As an Oracle Certified Professional Java SE 11 Developer, I specialize in building secure, scalable, and high-performing web applications.

    I graduated with a Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Electronics and Communication from the University Institute of Engineering and Technology, Panjab University, Chandigarh in 2017. My technical foundation grew stronger through internships at the National Institute of Technical Teachers Training & Research (2015) and the Terminal Ballistics Research Laboratory (2017).

    My career kicked off at Infosys (2017), where I sharpened my software development skills. In 2022, I joined Nagarro to tackle FinTech projects, diving into high-scale, transaction-heavy systems. Now, as a Senior Software Engineer at Exerp (since 2024), I’m enhancing fitness solutions in the health and wellness space.

    Beyond coding, I’m a tech educator and content creator. Through my Abhishek Verma YouTube channel, I share practical Java tutorials to make learning accessible and fun for developers worldwide, fostering a vibrant community.

    I’m passionate about free, quality education, delivering top-notch content on YouTube and Instagram. For those seeking personalized guidance, I offer 1:1 mentorship on Topmate at a nominal fee.
